The Best Movies of All Time

The best movies in the world, according to Miri

Manitou's Shoe

A German parody of western movies set in the Wild West. The movie is about a fictional Native American hero named Abahachi who is trying to save his tribe from being wiped out by the evil cowboys led by Santa Maria.

Hot Fuzz

A British action-comedy movie that is set in a small English village. The movie is about a London police officer who is transferred to a small village and discovers a series of mysterious deaths.

La La Land

An American romantic musical movie set in Los Angeles. The movie is about a jazz musician and an aspiring actress who meet and fall in love, and the challenges they face in their relationship as they pursue their dreams.

Other Great Movies

Shrek

An American animated comedy movie about an ogre who embarks on a journey to rescue a princess from a dragon.

Forrest Gump

An American drama movie about a man with a low IQ who witnesses and influences several historical events in the 20th century.
